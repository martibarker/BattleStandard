# Matched Play Guide — Warhammer: The Old World

**Reference**: WToW Matched Play (2025)

---

## Game Length

### Standard Game Length
- **Default**: 6 turns
- All matched play games use a standard 6-turn structure unless the Random Game Length rule is applied

### Random Game Length Rule
- Roll a d6 at the end of turn 5
- **1-3**: Game ends after turn 5 (5-turn game)
- **4-5**: Game continues to turn 6 (6-turn game)
- **6**: Game continues to turn 7 (7-turn game)

**Determining Winner**: At the end of the final turn, calculate total victory points (VP). Highest VP wins.

---

## Victory Points (VP)

### Primary Scoring

| Achievement | VP |
|---|---|
| **Destroy Enemy Unit** | 100 VP × Points Cost of unit / 1,000 |
| **Fleeing Caused** | 25 VP per model (even if not destroyed that turn) |
| **Hold Objective** | Depends on format (see below) |

*Example: Destroying a 500-point unit = 100 × (500/1,000) = 50 VP*

### Secondary Objectives
Each matched play format includes secondary objectives worth up to 20 VP each.

---

## Matched Play Formats

### Open War
**Purpose**: Flexible, casual play with minimal restrictions beyond faction composition rules

**Armies**: Any composition from faction book or Arcane Journal (within points limit)

**Secondary Objectives** (Choose 1):
- First Blood: Score 20 VP if you destroy an enemy unit before your opponent destroys one of yours
- Breakthrough: Score 20 VP if you have units within 12" of the enemy board edge at end of game
- Slay the Warlord: Score 20 VP if enemy general is destroyed

**No Victory Point Limit**: Armies can field any single unit regardless of army size

---

### Grand Melee
**Purpose**: Balanced, competitive 1v1 play with unit restrictions to prevent overwhelming single-model armies

**Armies**: Any composition (same as Open War)

**Grand Melee Restriction**:
- No single unit (character or entry) may cost more than **25% of total army points**
- Example at 2,000 points: no unit may cost more than 500 points
- Prevents degenerate lists with one overpowered unit

**Secondary Objectives** (Choose 1):
- Kingslayer: 20 VP if enemy general is destroyed
- Secure Flanks: 20 VP if you have units within 6" of both board edges at game end
- Rear Guard: 20 VP if you have units in enemy deployment zone at game end

**Battle Length**: Roll for random game length (5-7 turns)

---

### Combined Arms
**Purpose**: Competitive team play and restricted army list format with unit caps per category

**Armies**: Subject to composition AND unit identity limits (below)

**Unit Identity Limits** (Max number of identical units):
| Category | Max Identical Entries |
|---|---|
| Characters | 3 |
| Core | 4 |
| Special | 3 |
| Rare | 2 |

Example: You may include up to 4 entries of the same Core unit (e.g., 4× Spearmen units), but not 5.

**Secondary Objectives** (All 3 active):
- Control Center: 20 VP if you have units closer to the board center than enemy at game end
- Dominate Flanks: 20 VP if you have units in both enemy flanks (each flank = 6" from table edge)
- Break Their Lines: 20 VP if you have units within 12" of enemy deployment zone at game end

**Battle Length**: Standard 6-turn game (no random length)

## Scoring Summary Table

| Metric | VP Value |
|---|---|
| Unit destroyed | 100 × (unit cost / 1,000) |
| Model fled | 25 VP per model |
| Secondary objective (Open War) | 20 VP (1 of 3) |
| Secondary objectives (Combined Arms) | 20 VP each (all 3 active) |
| Secondary objectives (Grand Melee) | 20 VP (1 of 3) |

---

## Army Composition Rules

All matched play armies must follow faction composition rules (e.g., Core ≥25%, Characters ≤25%, etc.). Matched Play restrictions **stack on top of** composition rules.

---

## Game Setup Summary

1. **Choose Format**: Open War, Grand Melee, or Combined Arms
2. **Select Points Limit**: Typically 2,000 or 3,000 points
3. **Build Army**: Follow faction composition + matched play restrictions
4. **Determine Game Length**:
   - Open War / Grand Melee: Roll for random length (unless house-ruled to standard 6)
   - Combined Arms: Always 6 turns
5. **Roll for Deployment**: (Not covered in this guide)

---

## Key Differences at a Glance

| Feature | Open War | Grand Melee | Combined Arms |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Army Composition** | Faction rules only | Faction rules only | Faction rules + unit caps |
| **Unit Size Limit** | None | Max 25% army points | Max per category (see table) |
| **Game Length** | 5-7 turns (random) | 5-7 turns (random) | 6 turns (fixed) |
| **Secondary Objectives** | 1 of 3 | 1 of 3 | All 3 |
| **Competitive Tier** | Casual | Balanced | Competitive |

---

## Victory Determination

1. At the end of the final turn, tally all primary and secondary victory points
2. Player with highest total VP wins
3. If tied: Game is a draw (or use house rules for tiebreaker)
