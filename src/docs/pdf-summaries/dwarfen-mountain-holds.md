### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions

- rangers: 0-1 unit of Rangers may be taken as a Core choice
- bolt_thrower, grudge_thrower, cannon: 0-3 war machines chosen from Bolt Thrower, Grudge Thrower and Cannon per 1,000 points
- organ_gun, flame_cannon: 0-2 war machines chosen from Organ Gun and Flame Cannon per 1,000 points
