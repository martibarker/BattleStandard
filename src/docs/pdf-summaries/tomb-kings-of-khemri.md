## Tomb Kings of Khemri — Grand Army
**Source:** ravening_hordes

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min
- **Special:** 50% max
- **Rare:** 25% max
- **Mercenaries:** 20% max
- **Allies:** 25% max

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- tomb_guard, sepulchral_stalkers: 0-1 unit of Tomb Guard or Sepulchral Stalkers may be taken as a Core choice
- tomb_scorpion: 0-3 Tomb Scorpions per 1,000 points
- khemrian_warsphinx: 0-2 Khemrian Warsphinx (not counting character mounts) per 1,000 points
- screaming_skull_catapult: 0-2 Screaming Skull Catapults per 1,000 points
- casket_of_souls: 0-1 Casket of Souls per 1,000 points
