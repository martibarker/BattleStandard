## Orc & Goblin Tribes Grand Army
**Source:** ravening_hordes

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (orc_mobs, goblin_mobs, snotling_mobs, goblin_spider_rider_mobs, goblin_wolf_rider_mobs, night_goblin_mobs, night_goblin_squig_herds, black_orc_mobs)
- **Special:** 50% max (black_orc_mobs, troll_mobs, orc_boar_boy_mobs, orc_boar_chariots, goblin_wolf_chariots, snotling_pump_wagons, night_goblin_squig_hopper_mobs, goblin_bolt_throwas)
- **Rare:** 25% max (arachnarok_spiders, giants, mangler_squigs, goblin_rock_lobbers, doom_diver_catapults, troll_hag)
- **Mercenaries:** 20% max
- **Allies:** 25% max

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- night_goblin_mobs: 0-1 Night Goblin Mobs per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- night_goblin_squig_herds: 0-1 Night Goblin Squig Herd per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- night_goblin_squig_hopper_mobs: 0-1 Night Goblin Squig Hopper Mob per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- goblin_bolt_throwas: 0-2 Goblin Bolt Throwas per 1,000 points
- mangler_squigs: 0-1 Mangler Squig per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- goblin_rock_lobbers: 0-1 Goblin Rock Lobber per 1,000 points
- doom_diver_catapults: 0-1 Doom Diver Catapult per 1,000 points

## Orc & Goblin Tribes — Nomadic Waaagh!
**Source:** arcane_journal

### Special Abilities
- **Cunning Hunters:** Wolf Rider Mobs can have Ambushers special rule; Goblin Bosses/Shamans on Giant Wolf also get Ambushers
  - Affects: goblin_wolf_rider_mobs, goblin_warboss, goblin_bigboss, goblin_oddnob, goblin_oddgit
- **Hit 'em Fast and Hit 'em 'Ard!:** Orc models mounted on War Boar gain Impact Hits (1); 0-1 Boar Boy Mob per 1,000 pts may have Vanguard
  - Affects: orc_boar_boy_mobs, orc_warboss, orc_bigboss, orc_oddnob, orc_oddgit
- **Hunting Packs:** Wolf Rider Mobs can exchange Open Order/Skirmishers with Close Order/Horde
  - Affects: goblin_wolf_rider_mobs
- **On Da Move:** All characters must be mounted; effects adjusted for mounted status

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (goblin_wolf_rider_mobs, goblin_wolf_chariots, orc_boar_boy_mobs, black_orc_boar_chariots)
- **Special:** 50% max (orc_boar_boy_mobs, orc_boar_chariots, snotling_pump_wagons)
- **Rare:** 25% max (black_orc_boar_chariots, giants)
- **Mercenaries:** 25% max (badlands_ogre_bulls, bonegrinder_giants)

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- Standard composition rules

## Orc & Goblin Tribes — Troll Horde
**Source:** arcane_journal

### Special Abilities
- **Enhanced Regeneration:** Models with Regeneration may re-roll failed regen saves vs non-magical attacks
  - Affects: troll_mobs, troll_hag
- **Oil Dis Waw!:** Friendly Troll Mobs within command range of Shaman/Troll Hag can use that character's Leadership instead
  - Affects: troll_mobs
- **Troll Tongue:** Shamans in Troll Horde may know spells from Lore of Troll Magic in addition to normal lores
  - Affects: orc_weirdboy, orc_weirdnob, goblin_oddgit, goblin_oddnob

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 33% min (troll_mobs, orc_mobs, goblin_mobs, goblin_spider_rider_mobs, goblin_wolf_rider_mobs)
- **Special:** 50% max (troll_mobs, goblin_wolf_chariots, orc_boar_boy_mobs, orc_boar_chariots)
- **Rare:** 25% max (giants)
- **Mercenaries:** 25% max (badlands_ogre_bulls, bonegrinder_giants)

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- troll_mobs: 1 Troll Mob per 1,000 points
- orc_boar_boy_mobs: 0-1 Orc Boar Boy Mobs per 1,000 points
- orc_boar_chariots: 0-1 Orc Boar Chariots per 1,000 points
