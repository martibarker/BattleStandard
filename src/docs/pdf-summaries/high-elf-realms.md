## Grand Army of the High Elf Realms
**Source:** forces_of_fantasy

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min
- **Special:** 50% max
- **Rare:** 25% max
- **Mercenaries:** 20% max
- **Allies:** 25% max

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- prince, archmage: 0-1 High Elf Prince or Archmage per 1,000 points
- dragon_mage, handmaiden_of_the_everqueen: 0-1 Dragon Mage or Handmaiden of the Everqueen per 1,000 points
- dragon_princes_of_caledor: 0-1 unit of Dragon Princes of Caledor per 1,000 points
- flamespyre_phoenix, frostheart_phoenix: 0-1 Flamespyre or Frostheart Phoenix per 1,000 points (not counting character mounts)
- great_eagle: 0-2 Great Eagles per 1,000 points
- eagle_claw_bolt_thrower: 0-2 Eagle Claw Bolt Throwers per 1,000 points
