## Grand Army of the Empire
**Source:** forces_of_fantasy

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (veteran_state_troops, state_troops, state_missile_troops, free_company_militia, empire_archers, empire_knights)
- **Special:** 50% max (inner_circle_knights, empire_greatswords, pistoliers, outriders, demigryph_knights, great_cannon, mortar)
- **Rare:** 25% max (flagellants, helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery, empire_steam_tank)
- **Mercenaries:** 20% max
- **Allies:** 25% max

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- veteran_state_troops: 1 unit of Veteran State Troops per 1,000 points
- inner_circle_knights: 0-1 unit of Inner Circle Knights per 1,000 points
- great_cannon, mortar: 0-3 war machines chosen from Great Cannon and Mortar per 1,000 points
- helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery: 0-1 war machine chosen from Helblaster Volley Gun and Helstorm Rocket Battery per 1,000 points
- empire_steam_tank: 0-1 Steam Tank per 1,000 points

## Empire of Man — City-State of Nuln
**Source:** arcane_journal

### Special Abilities
- **Big Guns Know No Fear:** Empire Engineers (and units with them) gain Immune to Psychology while friendly war machine is in command range
  - Affects: empire_engineers, great_cannon, mortar, helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery, empire_steam_tank
- **Entrenched Position:** War machines may be entrenched during deployment; they gain cover bonus and can have obstacles. Lose benefit if they move.
  - Affects: great_cannon, mortar, helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery, empire_steam_tank
- **Gun Limbers:** War machines may gain Vanguard and Veteran special rules for points cost (+5 to +15 depending on weapon)
  - Affects: great_cannon, mortar, helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery
- **Handgun Drill:** Once per game, State Missile Troops detachments can fire with one additional rank
  - Affects: state_missile_troops
- **Veteran Outriders:** Outriders in this army can use missile weapons without the Ponderous penalty
  - Affects: outriders

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (nuln_state_troops, nuln_veteran_state_troops, nuln_state_missile_troops, outriders, free_company_militia, empire_archers)
- **Special:** 50% max (empire_greatswords, pistoliers, outriders, empire_knights, war_wagons, great_cannon, mortar)
- **Rare:** 25% max (empire_road_wardens, helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery, empire_steam_tank)
- **Mercenaries:** 25% max (imperial_ogres, imperial_dwarfs, doomseekers)

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- outriders: 0-1 unit of Outriders per 1,000 points
- great_cannon, mortar: 0-3 war machines chosen from Great Cannon and Mortar per 1,000 points
- helblaster_volley_gun, helstorm_rocket_battery, empire_steam_tank: 0-2 war machines chosen from Helblaster Volley Gun, Helstorm Rocket Battery and 0-1 Empire Steam Tank per 1,000 points
