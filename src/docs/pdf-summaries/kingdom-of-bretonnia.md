# Kingdom of Bretonnia - Army Compositions

**Sources:** Warhammer: The Old World — Forces of Fantasy (2023), Arcane Journal: Kingdom of Bretonnia (2023)

---

## Grand Army of Bretonnia
**Source:** forces_of_fantasy

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, men_at_arms, peasant_bowmen, knights_errant)
- **Special:** 50% max (squires, questing_knights, pegasus_knights, mounted_yeomen, battle_pilgrims)
- **Rare:** 25% max (grail_knights, field_trebuchet)
- **Mercenaries:** 20% max
- **Allies:** 25% max

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- battle_pilgrims: 0-2 units of Battle Pilgrims per 1,000 points
- field_trebuchet: 0-1 Field Trebuchet per 1,000 points

## Kingdom of Bretonnia — Errantry Crusade
**Source:** arcane_journal

### Special Abilities
- **Crusading Knights:** Characters and units gain Crusader's Zeal and can replace Knight's Vow with Crusader's Vow
  - Affects: baron, paladin, knights_errant, knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, battle_pilgrims, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, questing_knights, grail_knights, pegasus_knights, mounted_yeomen
- **Crusader's Zeal:** Units gain +1 to max charge range and Impetuous special rule
  - Affects: knights_errant, knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, battle_pilgrims, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, questing_knights, grail_knights, pegasus_knights, mounted_yeomen
- **Earn Your Spurs:** Knights Errant capturing enemy standards gain 100 VP; near Grail Vow holders or Lords, they can re-roll To Hit rolls of 1
  - Affects: knights_errant

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 33% min (knights_errant, battle_pilgrims, knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, squires, mounted_knights_of_the_realm)
- **Special:** 50% max (men_at_arms, peasant_bowmen, battle_pilgrims, questing_knights, pegasus_knights)
- **Rare:** 33% max (grail_knights, mounted_yeomen, field_trebuchet)
- **Mercenaries:** 25% max (empire_knights, inner_circle_knights)

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- knights_errant: 1+ units of Knights Errant per 1,000 points
- field_trebuchet: 0-1 Field Trebuchet (absolute maximum)

## Kingdom of Bretonnia — Bretonnian Exiles
**Source:** arcane_journal

### Special Abilities
- **Banished Lords:** Barons and Paladins cannot have Grail Vow; can replace Knight's Vow with Exile's Vow for free
  - Affects: baron, paladin, knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, knights_errant, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, pegasus_knights
- **The Exile's Vow:** Units gain Stubborn and Veteran; ignore panic from peasant units being destroyed nearby
  - Affects: baron, paladin, knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, knights_errant, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, pegasus_knights
- **Reclaimed Glory:** Cannot pray for Blessings at game start, but gain it automatically when making overrun or pursuing fleeing enemies

### Category Restrictions
- **Characters:** 50% max
- **Core:** 25% min (knights_of_the_realm_on_foot, mounted_knights_of_the_realm, yeoman_guard, peasant_bowmen, knights_errant, men_at_arms, mounted_yeomen)
- **Special:** 33% max (squires, pegasus_knights, battle_pilgrims)
- **Rare:** 33% max (questing_knights, border_princes_brigands, field_trebuchet, border_princes_bombard)
- **Mercenaries:** 25% max (free_company_militia, empire_archers)

### Per-1000-Points Limits & Unit Restrictions
- baron: 0-1 Baron per 1,000 points
- knights_errant: 0-1 units of Knights Errant per 1,000 points
- battle_pilgrims: 0-1 units of Battle Pilgrims per 1,000 points
- field_trebuchet: 0-1 Field Trebuchet per 1,000 points
- border_princes_bombard: 0-1 Border Princes Bombard per 1,000 points
